Who Are We

The Problem

Authorised Push Payment Fraud (APP) continues to rise despite the additional defenses UK banks have deployed, it accounted for 75% of all digital banking fraud during the first half of 2022 (Outseer Fraud & Payments Report: 1H 2022) and increased 39% in the same year (2022 UK Finance report).

The most vulnerable are disproportionately impacted by romance scams, purchase and investment scams and other types of social engineering.

Our Solution

We believe that by creating a new trusted network, banks can safely and securely exchange information to help stop scams before they happen. Banks will be able to match customers and exchange data flags relating to vulnerability, allowing them to proactively protect customers.

We assure unparalleled protection of sensitive customer data, facilitating a unique capability for institutions to confirm customer matches without the need to reveal any private information. This innovative approach ensures that sensitive data remains within the institution's secure network, effectively mitigating data protection risks and safeguarding customer privacy like never before.

What We Are

Our privacy-by-design software enables direct peer-to-peer customer matching and information exchange between organisations.

Distributed

We create a peer-to-peer network of organisations which allow them to exchange information through cloud based services and distributed event streaming technology.

Private

Clients get full assurance that sensitive customer data is better protected than ever before, allowing them to prove they have the same customer without disclosing any private data, because sensitive data never leaves their network, thereby reducing GDPR risks. We do this through our proprietary zero knowledge proof matching algorithms.

Intelligent

Our proprietary zero knowledge proof fuzzy matching algorithms are at the heart of our service, designed to securely and accurately match customer data across financial institutions, despite common challenges such as linguistic variations in names, outdated contact details, and inconsistently populated fields. These algorithms, protected by patents, enable complex matching processes that are not only highly effective in identifying customer overlaps but are also fully traceable, supporting audit requirements.

Fast

We use the latest cutting edge technology and design paradigms in order to provide the fastest concurrent exchange of information possible, while being highly scalable.

Validated

Our prototype was used successfully by banks in an 2019 FCA TechSprint, sponsored by the Financial Regulatory Authority. We worked with 5 banks, to help exchange data to spot anomalies across a network.

We have also worked closely with EY in their 2023 Innovation Lab where we rigorously tested our product and tailored it to real world needs faced by the industry
