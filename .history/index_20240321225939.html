The Problem

Fraud in the UK, and in particular Authorised Push Payment Fraud (APP), is a huge problem for UK banks. It costs hundreds of millions each year and causes distress and misery for thousands of customers. Often the most vulnerable in our society are worst affected.

Sharing intelligence between banks on fraudsters and their victims can really help. However, existing data sharing arrangements can be slow, siloed and expensive, additionally matching customers usually requires sharing sensitive personal data with an intermediary. Our solution offers industry a new alternative...

Our Solution

Our solution enables banks to match customers directly between themselves and then exchange customer-specific intelligence to help combat fraud.

This is all done in real time, and without ever having to share customer's sensitive personal data - providing an unparalleled level of data protection.

    We offer:
  • Bank-to-bank, real time, customer matching - achieved using our proprietary zero knowledge proof (ZKP) matching algorithms
  • Bank-to-bank, real time, data and intelligence exchange - fast enough to support fraud decisioning models and payments authorisation
  • Bank-to-bank, real time, customer specific dialogue and text exchange - helping banks' fraud prevention teams co-operate in protecting their customers

What We Are

Our privacy-by-design software enables direct peer-to-peer customer matching and information exchange between organisations.

Distributed

We create a peer-to-peer trusted network of organisations which allows them to match customers and exchange information through cloud based services and distributed event streaming technology.

Secure

Sensitive customer personal data never has to leave your secure domain.

Customer matching is done using an array of cryptographic hashes, and our proprietary zero knowledge proof (ZKP) matching algorithms.

Auditable

The logic used to determine a customer match across the network is fully traceable and auditable.

We understand how important it is for clients to be able to justify decisions and their impact on customer outcomes. We don't use complex 'black box' models.

Intelligent

Our proprietary fuzzy matching algorithms are at the heart of our solution and are protected by a patent. We can match customer data across financial institutions, quickly and accurately. The fuzzy logic component means we can overcome common challenges such as poor data quality, linguistic variations in names, and outdated contact details.

Fast

We use the latest cutting-edge technology and design paradigms in order to provide the fastest concurrent exchange of information possible, while being highly scalable.
