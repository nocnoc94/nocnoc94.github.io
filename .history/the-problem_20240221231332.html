The Problem

Authorized Push Payment (APP) fraud has emerged as a formidable challenge within the digital banking sector, significantly impacting consumers and financial institutions alike. This type of fraud involves deceiving individuals into voluntarily sending money to accounts controlled by criminals, often under false pretenses.

Recent data underscores the severity of the issue. The Outseer Fraud & Payments Report for the first half of 2022 highlighted that APP fraud accounted for 75% of all digital banking fraud. Furthermore, the 2022 UK Finance report indicated a 39% increase in APP fraud incidents within the same year, signaling a rising trend that poses a persistent threat to the safety and security of the UK financial system.
